Rules of Engagement

  • Kindness and authenticity
  • Taking people's stories seriously and cherishing them when you receive them
  • No mockery, demeaning vibes (i.e. eye rolling)
  • Only telling stories connected to you (i.e. don't challenge or correct other people's stories)
